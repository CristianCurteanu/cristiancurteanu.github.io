Build an MVP

As a startup you need a reliable product fast. Thanks to Cloud technologies, a maintainable MVP can be build in no time, and still leverage security, reliability, and small operational costs.

Maintain and Extend

As a startup, there is a constant need to pivot fast to another version of a product. Most of the times, a monolith application, especially poorly designed, will require more time to iterate to another version. On the other hand, if you need to iterate fast on your software features, Cloud technologies can be a good choice

Automate and Optimize

It is always good for a company to have a big part of processes automated, and to have low operational costs. With AWS Cloud technologies, both are real, since there are good tools to make all product processes automated, and leverage on demand billing for your service to operate
